Building your first Flutter app


Step 1

Start Android Studio and then click "Create New Flutter Project" on the welcome screen. Or if the Android Studio is already running without the Welcome screen, then click "File -> New -> New Flutter Project".

Step 2

Select "Flutter Application" if not already selected and then click NEXT

Step 3

Give a name for your Flutter Project.

Step 4

You can change the package name here, but for the time being leave it as it is and click "FINISH"

Step 5

Step 6

If your Android Virtual Device (AVD) is not running, run it by clicking Tools -> AVD Manager and then selecting the green play button on the emulator image. Wait till the AVD is fully loaded and ready.

Step 7

Click the Play button on the Android Studio toolbar and run the app in the emulator. When you are running for the app for the first time, this could take a couple of minutes. If there are no problems in the app, the app should start running on the emulator.
